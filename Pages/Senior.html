Career Advisor

Project Description

The website serves as an interactive platform integrated into a university's website, aiming to assist students in selecting suitable majors based on their interests, hobbies, and personality traits. Through a combination of chatbot technology and virtual reality (VR) experiences, students can explore various majors, learn about course offerings, and gain insights into study plans.

Admin Side

Technologies Used

  • React
  • React Hooks
  • API Integration
  • Responsive

Project Features

  1. Chatbot Integration:
    • The website features an advanced chatbot powered by ChatGPT, capable of engaging with students in natural language conversations.
    • The chatbot provides comprehensive information about the university's courses, majors, study plans, and academic offerings.
  2. Virtual Reality (VR) Experience:
    • Students can immerse themselves in virtual environments representing different academic disciplines and campus facilities.
    • VR simulations allow students to visualize potential career paths and understand the real-world applications of different majors.
  3. Speech to text and text to Speech Functionality:
    • The chatbot features speech-to-text and text-to-speech capabilities, allowing users to interact with the platform using voice commands.
    • Users can ask questions verbally, and the chatbot will convert the spoken words into text, providing responses audibly.
    • This functionality enhances user accessibility and offers an intuitive way to engage with the platform, particularly for users who prefer voice interactions.
  4. Personalized Guidance:
    • Through interactive dialogue, the chatbot guides students through a series of questions to identify their interests, hobbies, and personality traits.
    • Based on student responses, personalized recommendations for suitable majors are provided, taking into account individual preferences and strengths.
  5. Comprehensive Major Information:
    • Detailed information is available for each major, including curriculum details, course descriptions, total credits required, and potential career opportunities.
    • Students can explore the structure of study plans for various majors, understanding the sequence of courses and elective options available.
  6. User-Friendly Interface:
    • The website features an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for students to navigate and access relevant information.
    • Clear navigation menus and interactive elements ensure a seamless browsing experience for users of all levels of technical proficiency.
  7. Integration with University Website:
    • Seamlessly integrated into the university's existing website, ensuring continuity in branding and user experience.
    • The website maintains consistency with the university's design language and offers a cohesive online presence.
  8. Feedback Mechanism:
    • Students have the option to provide feedback on the chatbot interactions and VR experiences, helping to improve the platform over time.
    • Feedback mechanisms are incorporated to gather insights into user preferences and enhance the overall user experience.
  9. Accessibility and Compatibility:
    • The website is designed to be accessible to all users, including those with disabilities, with features such as screen reader compatibility and keyboard navigation.
    • Compatibility across devices and browsers ensures that students can access the platform from desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.
